A little bit about me.

I live in Toronto's vibrant downtown core and recently broke into the world of tech. I graduated with a degree in engineering that subsequently led to me into the workforce of cosmetic manufacturing. With a strong technical aptitude - I was narrowing into a very specified line of work that I wasn't sure I saw myself in long-term. At the same time, I was craving ways to expand my right-brain, picking-up hobbies like stand-up comedy. But of course you don't necessarily 'pick-up' stand-up as a hobby very easily.

As I pushed to document this experience through creative non-fiction writing, a Blog seemed appropriate. And that's when I discovered how compatible web development was with my interests and skills. I found myself fixated on understanding different website operations and to my own surprise, gained vested interest in programming. You really have to love challenges and continuous learning to work in this ever-evolving industry, which for me seemed like a no-brainer. Or should I say, an 'all-brainer', both left & right?!

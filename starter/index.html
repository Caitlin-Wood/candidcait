A little bit about me.

I live in Toronto's vibrant downtown core and recently broke into the world of tech. I graduated with a degree in engineering (proudly with first class honours) that subsequently led to me into the workforce of cosmetic manufacturing. With a strong technical aptitude - I was narrowing into a very specified line of work that I wasn't sure I saw myself in long-term. At the same time, I was

craving ways to expand my right-brain, picking-up hobbies like stand-up comedy. Of course you don't 'pick-up' stand-up as a hobby, so as I pushed to document this experience through creative non-fiction writing, a Blog seemed appropriate. And that's when I discovered how compatible web design was with my interests and skills. Finally, a place for both sides of my technical & creative brain!
